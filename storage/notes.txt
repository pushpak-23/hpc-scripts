Key Notes:

    pvcreate: Creates a physical volume from a block device.
    vgcreate: Creates a volume group from one or more physical volumes.
    lvcreate: Creates a logical volume within a volume group.
    lvextend: Expands the size of an existing logical volume.
    resize2fs: Resizes the filesystem on a logical volume to utilize the extended space.
    lvremove: Removes a logical volume (be cautious, as this deletes all data on the LV).
